Research

My research interests lie in the field of planning, scheduling, decision making under uncertainty, machine learning and artificial intelligence. Below you can find an overview of recent projects.

Planning and scheduling in smart grids

My work focuses on planning and scheduling algorithms to control flexible loads in distribution grids, exploiting stochastic information on power generation and consumption. My research is part of the GCP project and the NWO URSES program. More info:

Smoover

We developed reinforcement learning algorithms to compute speed limits for highways. These algorithms have been used to support the development of Smoover, a new Intelligent Transportation System in the Netherlands which aims to reduce traffic congestion. More info:
